Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • Cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Lasagna noodels
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Make meat sauce: Cook ground beef, onion, garlic, tomato sauce, paste, water, sugar, Italian seasoning, salt, pepper; simmer.
  3. Cook lasagna noodles; drain.
  4. Mix ricotta, egg, parsley, salt, pepper.
  5. Layer lasagna: sauce, noodles, ricotta mix, mozzarella, Parmesan; repeat.
  6. Cover with foil, bake 25 mins.
  7. Remove foil, bake 25 more mins until golden.
  8. Let stand 10 mins before serving.
