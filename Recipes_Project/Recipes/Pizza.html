Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Cheese
  • Dough
  • Basil leafs
  • Tomato sauce
  • Olive oil
  1. Prepare the Dough: Mix flour, water, salt, and yeast. Knead the dough until smooth. Then let it rise.
  2. Make the Sauce: Sauté finely chopped garlic and shallots in olive oil. Add diced tomatoes, cook until they turn into a sauce. Season with salt, pepper, tomato paste, and oregano. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  3. Prepare Toppings: For Marinara, use the prepared sauce. For Margherita, add fresh mozzarella slices and basil leaves on top of the sauce.
  4. Bake: Preheat the oven to 220°C. Stretch the dough, add toppings, and bake until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly. Optionally, drizzle with high-quality olive oil after baking.
