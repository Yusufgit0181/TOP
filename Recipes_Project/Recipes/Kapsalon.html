Kapsalon

Ingredients:

  • Doner
  • Chips
  • Garlic sauce
  • Hot sauce
  • Vegetables
  1. Prepare the fries by either frying them or baking them in the oven. You can use oven fries for a healthier option.
  2. Season the meat, typically kebab or shoarma, with spices like cayenne pepper and shoarma seasoning, then cook it in a skillet until done.
  3. Assemble the kapsalon by layering the fries at the bottom of an aluminum container. Add the cooked meat on top.
  4. Sprinkle grated cheese over the meat and fries. It's recommended to use mature Gouda cheese for perfect melting.
  5. Place the assembled kapsalon under a grill until the cheese is melted and bubbly
  6. Serve the kapsalon with salad greens on top and drizzle with garlic sauce and optionally, Hot sauce for added flavor
